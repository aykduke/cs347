FAQ

Is Korean related to Japanese or Chinese?

Korean shares somes words with Japanese and Chinese as a result of close historical interaction, but Korean is a language isolate and ultimately not related to either.

Is learning Korean hard?

It is actually very easy to read Korean, in fact you can learn it in a day because of its simple design. However, the rest of Korean can be difficult for English speakers because it uses honorifics, an extensive particle system, and a subject-object-verb order which is not present in English.

Do they speak English in Korea?

English is taught as a second language in Korea. Many signs are written in English along with romanized Korean.
